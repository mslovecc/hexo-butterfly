關於自己

Never put off till tomorrow what you can do today.
—— Thomas Jefferson

Hello Friends,

Thanks for dropping by and hope you are doing well. My name is Edwin Wong and my Chinese name is Ming. Most people call me MingGor, which means brother Ming. I can be your trustworthy and efficient sourcing agent in China.

For the time being, the whole world is suffering from the Covid-19 pandemic. Many factories and small businesses are closing and many people are losing their jobs or being furloughed. We have to find a way to make our own bread and I am willing to trade my service for the bread.

Guangzhou is known as the wholesale market of south China, I can be your sourcing agent to help you do the sourcing, inspection or even price negotiation with the suppliers. You can just sit in the office without risking yourself exposed to the Covid-19 virus.

It is the worst of times, it is the best of times. After the pandemic is over, our lives will be better. Hope we can make it through the Covid-19 pandemic together.
